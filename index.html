Educational Qualifications & Admission

Students must have a recognized high school certificate for undergraduate studies or a bachelor’s degree for postgraduate programs[cite: 4]. We accept national certificates like WAEC, NECO, KCSE, and ZIMSEC[cite: 6].

Visa & Costs

Average tuition is between $1,500 and $8,000 per year[cite: 37]. Monthly living expenses range from $300 to $700 depending on the city[cite: 39].

Foreign Student Work Permit Information

ATTENTION: Working without a permit results in the cancellation of your residence permit[cite: 126].

  • Eligibility: Undergraduate students must complete their first year before applying for part-time work[cite: 122, 125]. Graduate students can apply immediately[cite: 122].
  • Application: The employer must apply via the e-permit system; students cannot apply individually[cite: 123, 145].
  • Timeline: Work permits are usually issued within 30 days[cite: 219].
