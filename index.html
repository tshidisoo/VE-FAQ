Yes, parents or legal guardians can sponsor you. They will need to provide a notarized sponsorship letter and 3-6 months of bank statements.

You generally must pay an initial deposit (often the first semester's fee) to secure your official acceptance letter, which is required for the visa. The rest can be paid upon arrival or in installments.

It is recommended to bring at least $1,000 - $1,500 in cash to cover your first month's rent, deposit, residence permit fees, health insurance, and SIM card before you set up a local bank account.
